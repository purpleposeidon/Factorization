\title{The Logic Matrix Programmer\#{item.factorization:tool/matrix_programmer}}

The Logic Matrix Programmer (LMP) is the primary tool for Factorization.

It contains a repository of software,
and the data it caries is important and ubiquitous.

Being a vast trove of of data, an LMP can not be built from scratch.
They can often be recovered from ancient & unused devices.


Certain \link{socket}{socket parts} can be configured by clicking on them with the LMP.

Certain \link{servo}{Servo Instructions} can be configured by clicking on them with the LMP.
